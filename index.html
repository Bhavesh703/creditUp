Services We Offer

Service

Debt Counselling and Management

We aid you in intelligently strategizing planning your debt, whether it's minimizing costs, securing additional funds, or achieving a debt-free status. Our guidance covers every facet, offering support at every step of your financial planning.
Service

EMI Funding

Failing to make your EMI payment on time not only incurs significant penalties and interest charges but also adversely affects your credit score. Fortunately, we have a solution for you through EMI Funding.
Service

Fresh Beginning Loan

We are the first to believe in your journey, backing you as you move from being a reliable borrower, facing challenges as a defaulter, and ultimately regaining your status as a dependable borrower.
Service

Settlement Funding

If you have a strategy in place to contribute a portion of the (OTS) amount, ranging from 20% to 50%, from your own resources, we can step in to fund the remaining balance. This approach allows for a collaborative financing structure, where your initial investment serves as a foundation, and our funding covers the remaining percentage of the OTS amount. Moreover, the flexibility in the percentage you choose to self fund provides room for customization based on your financial capabilities and preferences.
Service

OTS One Time Settlement

In OTS transactions, substantial discounts of 70% to 80% are not uncommon, often accompanied by favorable payment terms. The outcome relies on effective legal representation and the exceptional negotiation skills of our qualified professionals.
Service

Debt Consolidation / Restructuring

If an excessive number of monthly obligations are causing stress on your budget, Debt Consolidation offers a solution to prevent payment delays. Through a mediation process, we negotiate to reduce interest rates, extend tenures, and implement balloon repayments, aligning them with your cash flow to avoid defaults. In extraordinary situations where default is inevitable, prioritizing damage minimization becomes crucial. Additionally, Repayment Holidays provide the necessary time to restore your cash flow to a stable state.
Service

Legal Support

Borrowers frequently make the error of signing loan documents without thorough review, and a majority of these agreements tend to favor the lender. In the event of a default, the borrower may encounter severe repercussions. Furthermore, the lender may enlist the services of a notorious collection agency and initiate multiple legal actions against the borrower experiencing financial distress. This is where our legal team becomes integral in managing and addressing such situations.
